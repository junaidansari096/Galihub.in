4. Shadow Ban System
VERY useful feature.

What is Shadow Ban?
The banned user thinks:

Their posts are public

Their comments are visible

But actually:

Nobody else sees them

Useful against:

Spammers

Trolls

Bot users

Shadow Ban Effects
Shadow-banned user:

Cannot trend

Cannot appear in search

Cannot gain reward points

Uploads auto-hidden
